==========
``rmcr.c``
==========

.. note:: 本文档翻译自 NuttX 官方文档，如需查阅最新版本请访问 https://nuttx.apache.org/docs/latest/

移除s all white space from the end of lines. Whitespace here includes space
characters, TAB characters, horizontal and vertical TABs, and carriage 返回.
Lines will be terminated with the newline character only.

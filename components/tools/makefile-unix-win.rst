=======================
``Makefile.[unix|win]``
=======================

.. note:: 本文档翻译自 NuttX 官方文档，如需查阅最新版本请访问 https://nuttx.apache.org/docs/latest/

Unix.mk is the Make文件 used when 构建ing NuttX in Unix-like systems.
It is selected from the top-level Make文件.

Win.mk is the Make文件 used when 构建ing natively under 窗口s.
It is selected from the top-level Make文件.
